An introduction to personal finance

The aim of this book of web pages is to introduce and document topics related to personal finance that working professional face in their day to day life. The goal is simple, a publically contributed project with provides platform for knowledge sharing of finance. Whatever you learn, just put it here in a github pull request in the template given here. Feel free to add upon any topic like what are the best mutual funds to invest in india?, or what and how taxes work?, or the benefits of taking personal loans.
